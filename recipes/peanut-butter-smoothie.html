Peanut Butter Smoothie

Description

This Peanut Butter Smoothie is quick and easy to jump start your day! Packed with energy, this Smoothie can also be vegan by replacing the milk with a dairy free alternative of your choice! This recipe makes about 500ml of Smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 2 frozen bananas
  • 2Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 Tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 tsp pea protein
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 cup milk / milk alternative

Steps

  1. Put everything into a blender.
  2. Blend.
  3. Serve.
