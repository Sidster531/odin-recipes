Lasagne

Description

This is a classic dish everyone should have in their repertoire. As a home cook or professional chef, knowing how to make a good lasagne is invaluable.

This recipe is both delicious, and easy to follow! It features ingredients most will have in their pantry, which makes it useful even for a regular Tuesday night.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Onion
  • 500g Ground Beef
  • 1 Can Tomato Mix
  • 2 Sachets Tomato Paste
  • 2 tsp Table Salt
  • 2 tsp Mince Spice
  • 2 tsp Mixed Herbs
  • 250g Lasagne Sheets
  • 750 ml Milk
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 250g Mozzarella
  • 250g Parmesan
  • 2 Tbsp Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius
  2. Fry onion, herbs, spice and salt in a large pan.
  3. When onions are golden brown, add mince to the pan.
  4. When the mince is cooked, add the tinned tomato.
  5. Simmer for 15 minutes
  6. Add tomato paste.
  7. Melt butter in a saucepan.
  8. Add flour to the butter to form a paste.
  9. Add milk.
  10. Let boil until it thickens.
  11. Add half the mozzarella and half the parmesan to the sauce.
  12. Once melted, set aside.
  13. In a large baking dish, put a layer of cheese sauce, topped with a layer of mince and add a layer of lasagne sheet.
  14. Repeat layering until the dish is full, ending with a layer of mince.
  15. Bake in the oven until pasta is soft.
  16. Add the remainder of the cheese to the top of the dish.
  17. Bake until cheese is golden brown.
