Cheesy Mashed Potato

Description

Cheesy Mashed Potato is a great side that goes with most meats. have it with your Sunday Lunch, or sausages and gravy. The versatility of this dish will have you needing this recipe multiple times a week!

Ingredients

  • 5 medium sized potatoes
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 200ml double cream
  • 125g Gouda
  • 125g Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Put water to boil on the stove.
  2. Peel potatoes and chop them into roughly 10 pieces per potato.
  3. Put potatoes into boiling water.
  4. When the potatoes are soft, drain the water and add the milk and cream.
  5. Mash the potatoes until smooth.
  6. Once smooth, add the cheeses and continue mashing.
  7. When the cheese has melted, serve piping hot.
